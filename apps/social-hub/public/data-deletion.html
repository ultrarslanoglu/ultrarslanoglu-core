About Data Deletion

When you request data deletion, we will:

  • Remove your OAuth access tokens
  • Delete your connected social media account information
  • Remove uploaded content and analytics data
  • Deactivate your account in our system

Note: This process may take up to 30 days to complete. You will receive a confirmation email once the deletion is finished.
