# Security Policy

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to us at security@ultrarslanoglu.com

We appreciate responsible disclosure and will respond promptly to security reports.

## Scope
- ultrarslanoglu.com
- All subdomains of ultrarslanoglu.com
- Social Media Hub application

## Out of Scope
- Third-party services (TikTok, Instagram, YouTube, X)
- Social engineering attacks
- Physical security

## Response Timeline
We aim to respond to security reports within 48 hours.
